Live quizzes you actually own.

QuizDock is an open-source, self-hosted live quiz platform — real-time multiplayer, players join by PIN or QR with no account, and your data never leaves your servers.

Everything for a live game, on your infra

⚡

Real-time multiplayer

Socket.IO game engine with authoritative server timing. Players join a session by 6-digit PIN or QR code — built for groups from a handful to a few hundred.

🏠

Self-hosted & sovereign

Runs entirely on your own infrastructure with Docker. No SaaS, no third party — the questions, the answers and the results stay on your servers.

🧩

Quiz builder

Seven question types — single/multi choice, true-false, text, numeric, reorder and poll — with media support and reusable quizzes.

🏆

Live leaderboard & podium

Time-weighted scoring with streak bonuses, a leaderboard between questions and a final podium projected on the big screen.

🎨

White-label at runtime

Rebrand name, logo and CSS through an env var and a mounted folder — no rebuild. Make every instance look like yours.

🎟️

No player accounts

Players are in with a tap — just a PIN or QR. No signup, no email, no profile to manage. Hosts can plug in OIDC when they need it.

Self-host in 2 minutes

Everything ships as containers. Clone, bring the stack up, open the app.

git clone https://github.com/fchaussin/quiz-dock.git
cd quiz-dock
docker compose up -d

# then open the app in your browser
open http://localhost:18081

Rebrand without rebuilding: set APP_NAME and drop a logo + CSS into the branding/ volume. See the repository README for production and OIDC setup.

How it works

  1. 1

    Host creates a session

    Build a quiz, hit present — QuizDock locks a server-side snapshot and shows a PIN.

  2. 2

    Players join by PIN or QR

    No account, no install. They pick a nickname and they are in the lobby instantly.

  3. 3

    Play, score, celebrate

    Answer in real time, watch the leaderboard move, and crown the podium at the end.
